            02 — How it works

            Four inputs, reasoned together, one report out.

            Each step feeds the next. Nothing is analyzed in isolation.

            01

            Analyze the store

            StoreSage crawls your catalog, pricing patterns, category structure, and the page elements that drive conversion — no manual setup required.

            02

            Find and study competitors

            Stores selling into the same niche are identified automatically and compared on pricing, product variety, discounting, and positioning.

            03

            Ask you a few questions

            A short AI-guided interview fills in what data alone can't show: your goals, your customers, your real constraints.

            04

            Generate the growth report

            All three inputs go into the reasoning engine, which returns competitor comparisons, detected weaknesses, and specific recommendations.

            03 — The discovery interview

            Context an analytics dashboard can't collect on its own.

            Before the report is built, StoreSage's chatbot asks about your goals, your customers, your pricing stance, and what's actually been getting in the way.

            StoreSageWhat's the main goal for the store right now — more traffic, higher conversion, or repeat customers?
            YouConversion. We get visitors but checkout drop-off is high.
            StoreSageGot it. How would you describe your pricing versus the two or three competitors you watch most closely?
            YouProbably a bit higher, but our product quality is better.

            Why this step matters

            Two stores can share almost identical metrics and still need opposite advice — one is scaling ad spend, the other is protecting margin. The interview is how StoreSage tells the difference before it writes a single recommendation.

            Every answer is stored and passed straight into the reasoning engine alongside the website and competitor data.

            04 — The output

            One structured report. Nothing left to interpret.

            Everything StoreSage finds is organized into three columns — what your competitors are doing, what's holding your store back, and what to do about it.

            Growth Strategy Report yourstore.com
            Competitor comparisons
            • 3 of 5 niche competitors run tiered bundle pricing you don't offer
            • Average competitor catalog is 40% larger
            • Two competitors run always-on free shipping thresholds
            Detected weaknesses
            • Checkout requires account creation before payment
            • Product pages lack size/fit guidance
            • No urgency or scarcity signals on high-traffic pages
            Strategic recommendations
            • Add guest checkout to reduce drop-off
            • Introduce a starter bundle to match competitor pricing tiers
            • Test a free-shipping threshold against current margin
